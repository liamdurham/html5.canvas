The "Big Three":

My personal favorite is Evike.com, but AirsoftMegastore and AirsoftGI both have awesome sales at times. Your favorite store could be a local retailer, or even a more specialized parts store, but all new players need to know about these three stores and the price reductions they offer. Don't be fooled, however; there are many cheaper places to get the same gear if you shop around. Use these stores as a guide or final resort, not as a main purchasing site.
